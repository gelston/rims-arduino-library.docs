Detailed Description

Toolkits for process identification to facilitate PID tunning.

It sends differents values to the SSR (0%->50%->100%->0%) and monitors the resulting temperature on the serial monitor. Open the monitor before stating identification. It last 30 min.

Author
Francis Gagnon

void RimsIdent::run ( )

Start identification procedure.

It will last 30 minutes. Step order is :

  1. 0-STEP1VALUE% at t = STEP1TIME sec
  2. 50%-100% at t = 10 min
  3. 100%-0% at t = 20 min
  4. end at t = 30 min

All information for process identification is printed in Serial monitor on Arduino IDE.

I won't explain here how to identify process and how to tune PID with that information but there's a lot on information here : http://www.controlguru.com/wp/p87.html
